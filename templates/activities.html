NRI to conduct Medical camp in kunchanapally


NRI university of medical sciences is going to organize free medical camps in kunchanapally and nearby rural areas and all the incharges of the village will be informed with the dates, people can access these services if they hold an aadhaar card or ration card, basic services will be offered for free any severe cases will be treated at the university only, please acknowledge and gain the best out of this oppurtunity
